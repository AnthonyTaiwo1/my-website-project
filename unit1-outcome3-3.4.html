UNIT 1: Outcome 3: 3.4

The way I would go about testing a digital project solution, is by allowing the desired target audience to look at it. They would essentially tell you what they like about it and what they do not like about it, and from that feedback you can change digital solution. The digital solution can undergo quality control to ensure that it fulfils all the necessary quality requirements. It is important to investigate the budget limitations, the industry standards and identify all the risks posed when testing. [9]
